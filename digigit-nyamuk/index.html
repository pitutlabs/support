Subscriptions

Digigit Nyamuk offers Premium Monthly and Premium Annual subscriptions through Apple. Subscriptions are managed by Apple and can be cancelled anytime in Settings.

  • Open the iOS Settings app.
  • Tap your Apple ID.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Select Digigit Nyamuk to manage or cancel your subscription.

Restore Purchases

If Premium is not showing after a reinstall or device change, open the paywall in Digigit Nyamuk and tap Restore Purchases.

Privacy

Digigit Nyamuk does not require an account. On supported devices, story generation runs on device. On other supported devices, cloud fallback avoids sending the child's real name to the AI provider and protects requests with Apple's App Attest.

Read the Digigit Nyamuk Privacy Policy.
