Digigit Nyamuk Support

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026

Digigit Nyamuk is a parent-facing iOS app from Pitut Labs that helps families create personalized bedtime stories from a child's daily emotions.

Information You Provide

The app lets a parent or guardian create profiles, choose daily emotions, and generate bedtime stories. The profile name can be any name, nickname, initials, or label you choose. You should not enter a child's real name if you are not comfortable doing so. App data may include the profile name or label, age, favorite character type, selected emotions, saved stories, and story preferences.

How Information Is Used

This information is used to provide app functionality, including generating personalized stories, saving story history, syncing supported app data across the parent's Apple devices, and restoring Premium subscription access.

On-Device and Cloud Processing

On supported devices, story generation can run on device. On other supported devices, Digigit Nyamuk may use a cloud fallback through a Pitut Labs service to generate a story. The cloud fallback is designed to avoid sending the entered profile name to the AI provider and protects requests with Apple's App Attest. Operational logs may be used to maintain reliability and prevent abuse.

Apple Services

Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple through StoreKit. iCloud may be used to sync supported app data through the parent's Apple account. Apple handles those services according to Apple's privacy terms.

No Advertising or Tracking

Digigit Nyamuk does not show third-party ads, does not sell personal information, and does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Data Choices

You can delete profiles and saved stories in the app. You can manage iCloud data from Apple's iCloud settings. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in the iOS Settings app under your Apple ID subscriptions.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, contact support@pitutlabs.com.
